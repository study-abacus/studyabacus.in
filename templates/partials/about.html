Welcome to Study Abacus

Team STUDY ABACUS set up by a group of professionals who are from different walks of life and were involved in various educational and socio-cultural activities in their own respective areas. All these like minded individuals decided to join together and serve the society.

Team STUDY ABACUS believes by imparting education to women and children will empower them to stand in the society on their own and to make them self-reliant to earn themselves improve and develop local educational and cultural programs and in doing so in turn provide better opportunities for children and women.


Mission

Our mission is to reach to maximum number of kids, train them to create a strong foundation for mathematics through abacus for our children with efficiency and accuracy.


Vision

Our vision is to grow nationally with continuous research & development and introducing high class programmes and trainings, keeping in mind the children of the Nation and building up relations with its business partners, franchises, employees etc.
